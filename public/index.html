Welcome to Yext Sites

Yext Sites makes it easy to create high-performance websites based on a Knowledge Graph.

It is an all-in-one platform that combines the cutting edge of a static-site-generation, a continuous deployment pipeline, and scalable hosting infrastructure – all in a single workflow that works out-of-the box.

Yext Sites is maniacally committed to the following:

  • Speed: Statically render every page and deploy them to the edge. Your pages load lightning fast, providing your customers with an incredible experience and your marketing team with amazing SEO.

  • Scalability: Generate hundreds of thousands of pages in minutes.Yext Sites is purpose-built to efficiently process thousands of data updates per second. Leverage the benefits of static-site-generation at enterprise-scale.

  • Simplicity: We want to make it as easy as possible to focus on creating incredible experiences for your customers, by streamlining the setup and configuration of enterprise-grade deployment and hosting infrastructure.
