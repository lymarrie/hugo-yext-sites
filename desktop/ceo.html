Luc Marrie - CEO

Luc Marrie is Chief Executive Officer of Luc's Electronics. Before being named CEO in March 2021, Luc held leadership roles in both Hitchhikers and Platform Services at Yext.

Joining Yext in 2020, he quickly became known as a leader who could span a breadth of technologies and businesses to transform some of Yext's biggest product offerings.

Most recently, Luc was executive vice president of Brian Leveque's AFT team. In this role he led the transformation of AFT deliveries, which outperformed the market and took share from competition.
